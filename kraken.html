The Kraken menu

The Kraken is a sea monster out of Norse and Scandinavian mythology. It seems like a gigantic squid or octopus.

The Kraken was said to live in the ocean waters off Norway and Greenland.

It was first mentioned in a fourteenth century Icelandic saga.

In Norwegian, kraken means "unhealthy animal". Similar English words are "crook" and "crank".

British science fiction and fantasy writer China Mieville wrote a novel called "Kraken" about a cult that worships the creature.

H. P. Lovecraft's famous novel "The Call of Cthulhu" details a creature similar to the Kraken. Tennyson wrote a sonnet about a monster resembling the Kraken.
