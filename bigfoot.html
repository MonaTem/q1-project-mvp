The Bigfoot or Sasquatch is a contemporary American legend.

Bigfoot is a large apelike creature that lives primarily in Pacific Northwest forests and was first noticed by native American tribes in the nineteenth century.

There have been many reported sightings of Bigfoot, although scientists are skeptical of its existence and believe many of the sightings to be hoaxes or misidentification.
