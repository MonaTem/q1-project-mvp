The Yeti or Abominable Snowman is a giant apelike creature said to live in the Himalayas.

It's supposed to exist in Tibet, Nepal, Bhutan, Russia, and India.

The name Yeti roughly translates from Tibetan as "Rock Bear".

Most of the sightings of Yeti and evidence of its existence have happened on K2, the world's second highest mountain.

Over the years, many scientists and mountaineers have searched for Yeti, or claim to have seen it, heard it, or found evidence of it. World famous mountain climbers Sir Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay were among them. A supposed Yeti footprint was found on Mount Everest during Hillary's 1951 Everest Expedition.
