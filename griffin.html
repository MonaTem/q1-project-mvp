The Griffin or Gryphon is a monster from ancient Egyptian, Persian, Greek, and Roman mythology.

It's a cross between a lion and an eagle.

It's shown in different ways--often with the body and tail of a lion, and the head, wings, and talons of an eagle on all four legs.

Sometimes it has the back legs of a lion.rm for any strange monster composed of parts of different animals or for anything fabulous, imaginary or unlikely.
