The Hippogriff is a legendary creature with the front half of an eagle and the back half of a horse.

Hippogriffs first showed up in Greek and Roman mythology.

A hippogriff named Buckbeak has an important role in the Harry Potter novels and movies. It is one of the creatures featured in Hagrid's "Care of Magical Creatures" class.
