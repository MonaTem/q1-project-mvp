The Manticore is a monster from Persian mythology. It's similar to the Egyptian sphinx, with a lion's body, a human head, and the tail of a porcupine or a scorpion's thumbnail .

A medieval version had a tiger's body, an old man's head, boar tusks, and spiral horns. It had three rows of sharp teeth. In some versions it had wings or a dragon's tail.

Its name in Persian meant "man-eater". The name comes from the Persian name for “Man eater”, Martyaxwar. In Latin it became Mantichora which in English morphed into Mantichore

In one of the "007" spy movies, the female villain owns a yacht called "Manticore".
